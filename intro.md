# EOSC CZ Summer School for Data Stewards 2026

Resources and materials for the training sessions conducted by [ScaDS.AI](https://scads.ai/) as part of the [EOSC CZ Summer School for Data Stewards 2026](https://www.eosc.cz/en/news-and-events/events-calendar/summer-school-for-data-stewards-2026)

> **These pages are still under construction. Please check back later.**

## Contents

### Preparation

[Instructions](preparation/readme.md) on how to prepare your e-INFRA CZ account and your laptop for the training sessions.

### Session 1: JupyterLab + AI assistant as Data Steward Workbench

Introducing data stewards to JupyterLab as a potential workbench for practical, reproducible workflows supporting a range of tasks (e.g. data intake and exploration, documentation, and DMP drafting), with an integrated AI assistant.

### Session 2: Python Toolbelt for Data Stewards in JupyterLab

Providing a practical, Python-based toolkit for data stewards to turn incoming project data into a validated, well-documented, and handover- or deposit-ready package, using JupyterLab as the execution and reporting environment.
