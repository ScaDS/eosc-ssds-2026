# EOSC CZ Summer School for Data Stewards 2026

Resources and materials for the training sessions conducted by [ScaDS.AI](https://scads.ai/) as part of the [EOSC CZ Summer School for Data Stewards 2026](https://www.eosc.cz/en/news-and-events/events-calendar/summer-school-for-data-stewards-2026)

## Contents

### Preparation

[Instructions](preparation/readme.md) on how to prepare your e-INFRA CZ account and your laptop for the training sessions.

### Session 1

[JupyterLab + AI assistant as Data Steward Workbench](session1/readme.md): Introducing data stewards to JupyterLab as a potential workbench for practical, reproducible workflows supporting a range of tasks (e.g. data intake and exploration, documentation, and DMP drafting), with an integrated AI assistant.

Slides for this session can be downloaded as pdf [here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ScaDS/eosc-ssds-2026/main/session1/slides/session-1.pdf).

### Session 2

[Python Toolbelt for Data Stewards in JupyterLab](session2/readme.md): Providing a practical, Python-based toolkit for data stewards to turn incoming project data into a validated, well-documented, and handover- or deposit-ready package, using JupyterLab as the execution and reporting environment.

Slides for this session can be downloaded as pdf [here](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/ScaDS/eosc-ssds-2026/main/session2/slides/session-2.pdf).

### Practical Exercises

The how-to and the notebooks for the [practical exercises](practise/readme.md).
